About Megan

Hello! My name is Megan, and I'm passionate about creating custom Sims builds. I've been playing The Sims since its early days and have always enjoyed sharing my creations with fellow players.

On this website, you'll find a collection of my favorite builds, ranging from cozy starter homes to extravagant mansions and community lots. I hope you enjoy them as much as I enjoyed creating them. Feel free to download and use them in your game, and don't forget to share your experience with me on social media!
